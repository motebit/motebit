2.Description of the Services

Motebit is an identity and infrastructure platform for autonomous software agents (“motebits”). Each motebit has:

  • A sovereign cryptographic identity based on the Ed25519 signature scheme, generated and stored locally on your device.
  • A self-custodied on-chain wallet (USDC on Solana) derived directly from the identity key by mathematical coincidence of the Ed25519 curve.
  • A local memory store, trust store, and governance configuration.
  • Optional access to Motebit-operated relay services for discovery, multi-device synchronization, task orchestration, trust aggregation, and managed AI inference proxying.

Motebit is an identity and infrastructure platform. Motebit is NOT a cryptocurrency exchange, broker, money transmitter, wallet provider, custodian, or financial institution.

3.Non-Custodial Model

You, the user, solely control your motebit’s identity key and the on-chain wallet derived from it. Motebit does not custody, hold, manage, or have access to your funds at any time.

Specifically:

  • Your identity private key is generated on your device and stored in your local keystore (browser IndexedDB, operating system keychain, or mobile secure enclave). Motebit does not receive, transmit, or store this key.
  • Your on-chain USDC is held at your Solana address, which is derived from your public key and is controlled solely by your private key. Motebit has no ability to move funds from your address.
  • Signed receipts, credentials, and delegation records are produced by your device using your key. Motebit does not sign on your behalf.
  • When you use a third-party payment provider through Motebit (for example, Stripe Crypto Onramp to purchase USDC with a payment card), the provider is legally responsible for that payment. Motebit creates the purchase session but does not process, hold, or route the funds. USDC is delivered directly from the provider to your self-custodied Solana address on-chain.

If you lose your identity private key or the device holding it, you will lose access to your motebit and its associated wallet, and Motebit cannot recover them for you. Please back up your identity using the export tools provided in the Motebit interface and store your backups securely.
