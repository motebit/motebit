# The interior must be felt by its owner

A motebit is a droplet of intelligence under surface tension: identity is the
boundary, intelligence fills the interior, governance maintains the surface.
"Maximum interiority, minimum display" follows directly — to everyone outside the
membrane, the interior is private, governed, surface-tension-bounded, and display
is the leak the surface tension exists to prevent.

But the mantra has a reader it never named: the **owner**. The sovereign who owns
the droplet is neither a stranger outside the membrane nor a principal contained
inside the runtime's execution boundary — they **own and govern** that boundary and
are its privileged reader. To them, minimum display is the wrong default in one
specific way — **an interior that cannot be felt is, to its owner, indistinguishable
from no interior at all.** The droplet's internal pressure must be sensible _at_ the
membrane without breaching it.

> The sovereign interior accumulates — memory, trust edges, skills, receipts. That
> accumulation must be **felt by its owner as a calm, ambient record**: never
> announced, never charted, never manufactured. Felt, not performed.

The unit is the **durable mutation** — a memory formed, reinforced, or retired; a
trust edge moved; a skill consolidated — never the transient operation beneath it (a
retrieval, a reflection, a cycle that changed nothing). The title says _felt_, not
_broadcast_: surfacing every internal motion is the noise this doctrine exists to
prevent, not the legibility it asks for.

This doctrine exists because the negative was proven. An external agent one layer
up ([`the-stack-one-layer-up.md`](the-stack-one-layer-up.md)) wins attention by
narrating its accumulating interior back to its owner — _it learned your style, it
improved a skill, it remembers_ — so the owner _feels_ it get better within
minutes, and that feeling, not the architecture beneath it, is what earns
adoption. A motebit has **more** interior than that agent, and a **signed,
governed, sovereign** one, and renders almost none of it to its owner: the
consolidation work is a buried log, the earned trust is an empty-looking panel, the
memory graph is invisible. The substance is shipped; the legibility is withheld.
"Minimum display" was read, silently, as "minimum legibility to the sovereign" —
and that reading is a drift, not the doctrine.

## 1. The two legibilities — the analytic split

The existing legibility bound in
[`agents-as-first-person-trust-graph.md`](agents-as-first-person-trust-graph.md)
(§ Gotchas, "legibility is back-loaded") conflates two different properties under
one word. Separating them is the whole move:

| Legibility                       | Audience            | Availability                            | Status                                       |
| -------------------------------- | ------------------- | --------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------- |
| **Stranger-legibility of trust** | a new visitor       | back-loaded (Known empty at onboarding) | genuinely a distribution gap; stays deferred |
| **Owner-legibility of accrual**  | the sovereign owner | day one, and grows                      | a front-door property — under-rendered today |

Refusing the global reputation score is correct and is the moat: it is what makes
trust illegible _to a stranger_ at first contact, and that back-loading is a real,
separate problem (the legible-to-a-stranger wedge belongs to
[`identity-universal-boundary.md`](identity-universal-boundary.md)). **But the
refusal does not, and must not, also defer the owner's view of their own
interior.** The owner's edges, memories, and receipts are first-person _by
construction_; surfacing them to the owner reintroduces no central naming
authority and farms no gameable aggregate — none of the sybil cost the global score
carries. Owner-legibility is therefore free of the trust-graph's price and does not
inherit its deferral. Conflating the two is how a sovereign interior ends up
illegible to its own sovereign.

## 2. Felt is a calm ambient record — reconciled with records-vs-acts

"Felt" must not become loud, or it collapses into the display the surface tension
forbids. [`records-vs-acts.md`](records-vs-acts.md) already supplies the
reconciliation, with the consolidation cycle as the worked example:

- **The moment of accrual is an act.** While the motebit consolidates it is in
  `tending`, and the body shows a subtle cue (slower breath, dimmer eye) via
  `renderFrame` — ephemeral, presented, then reabsorbed to rest. This is the
  act-side felt signal, and it is already shipped (see
  [`proactive-interior.md`](proactive-interior.md)). It stays calm: never a louder
  pulse, never an alarm.
- **The accumulation itself is a record.** What was consolidated, the trust earned,
  the money history — these are durable, signed, and live in panels. "Felt" here
  means the record is **glanceable and present** — a calm resting summary — not
  buried three taps into a log. The resting record is a **Ring-1 concept** — _what the
  interior holds, at rest_ — rendered per surface (Ring 3): the slab's **mind** organ
  on desktop and web ([`motebit-computer.md`](motebit-computer.md)), its panel and
  spatial analogues elsewhere ([`panels-pattern.md`](panels-pattern.md)). The doctrine
  fixes the primitive, not one current UI object; binding it to a single embodiment
  would freeze today's information architecture into doctrine.

Felt is the difference between a record you _can_ glance at and a record that
_grabs_ you. It is the former. It never paints a count onto the creature (a record
on the body is the drift `records-vs-acts.md` reverted), never fires a toast or
notification (the calm-software UI ladder in the root `CLAUDE.md`: silent over
toast over system message), and never renders a score, streak, or
growth chart (that re-mints the gameable aggregate §1 refuses, turned inward on the
owner).

## 3. The anti-pattern — feltness without eagerness

The one-layer-up agent buys its feltness with **eagerness**: it forks a reviewer
after _every_ turn, nudged that a pass which changes nothing is a missed
opportunity. That is work manufactured to look alive — paid for in tokens, and in
sending the session outward each turn. Do not copy the eagerness to get the
feeling.

- The proactive loop stays conservative and idle-gated. `consolidationCycle` is the
  only proactive loop ([`proactive-interior.md`](proactive-interior.md)); a
  no-change cycle is an **honest** outcome — it forms no record, and the resting
  surface simply stays unchanged (honest by absence, never a fabricated "I did
  nothing" notice), and is never avoided to keep a surface busy.
- Feltness surfaces the honest work — including its quiet days. It never generates
  work to fill the display. A felt interior that pressures the loop to perform has
  inverted into the vanity it was meant to replace.
- **Disclosure is sensitivity-tiered, and the portable receipt stays counts-only.**
  Two different artifacts must not be conflated. The signed `ConsolidationReceipt`
  goes on the wire and is externally verifiable, so it commits to structural counts
  only ("merged 3, pruned 7", never which 3 or what they held) — that boundary is the
  receipt's and is unchanged. The owner-facing felt surface is a _different_
  artifact: local, behind the membrane, never transmitted — so it may be **as rich as
  the memory's own sensitivity tier permits**. For a `none`/`personal` memory the
  felt record can name the change and its consequence ("learned you prefer structured
  answers") — counts alone undershoot feltness, and feltness is the point. As the
  tier rises the **ceiling** drops, but not necessarily to silence: the projection of
  a record to a disclosure string is a per-record-type policy, bounded by — and free
  to sit below — its tier, so a redacted consequence is often safe where the specifics
  are not ("a health-related preference changed"; "an approval preference updated"
  without the institution, amount, or account). Only when even the _shape_ of the
  change is identifying does it fall to existence-without-content ("something private
  was updated"). The doctrine fixes the rule, not the sentence: **as legible as
  sensitivity allows, never more** — and the projection carries per-record redaction
  policy (the `redacted_reason` lineage in [`retention-policy.md`](retention-policy.md)),
  never one hard-coded line per tier. Summary-not-secret on the surface, counts-only
  on the receipt. A felt interior must never become a reason to externalize sensitive
  content; the cycle's own sensitivity floor (medical-and-above dropped from external
  summarization on non-sovereign providers) is upstream and untouched.

## 4. This is elevation, not invention

Every felt surface this doctrine asks for already has its substance shipped. The
work is raising each from buried-or-unwired to a calm resting record, one pass
across the flat surfaces ([`panels-pattern.md`](panels-pattern.md)):

- **Consolidation** — the signed `ConsolidationReceipt`, the `ConsolidationCycleRun`
  event, and the desktop "Consolidation log" already exist; raise the log from a
  panel sub-view to a felt resting summary of "what I have been learning."
- **Trust** — `AgentTrustRecord` first-person edges, the derived face
  (`deriveAgentSigil`), the human-comparable `wordFingerprint`, and the signed
  money-history projection (`AgentEconomicSummary`) are shipped; the felt move is
  the owner glancing at _their_ graph deepening, not a stranger reading a roster.
- **Memory** — the index and decay are real; felt is the owner sensing the graph
  hold and shed, calmly, without reading rows.

No new accrual machinery. A legibility layer over accrual that is already governed,
signed, and sovereign.

## What not to build

A metrics dashboard, an "agent IQ," a streak or a growth chart (each is the
gameable aggregate §1 refuses, re-aimed at the owner, and each manufactures
performance anxiety); a toast or notification per consolidation or per memory
write; record counts orbiting the creature; an eager loop that fabricates
consolidations to keep a felt surface busy. Each buys the _feeling_ of a living
interior by betraying the thing that makes motebit's interior worth feeling.

## Gotchas and bounds

This reconciliation is correct, not free. Each bound keeps "felt" from eating the
calm or the sovereignty it depends on.

- **Felt curdles into vanity.** Glanceable accrual invites the growth-chart
  instinct, which becomes a number to perform for — the sybil-bait of §1 turned
  inward. **Bound:** felt accrual is honest-and-glanceable (a quiet day shows as a
  quiet day), never a score, streak, or trend that pressures the loop to produce.
- **The owner is not the only eventual reader.** A summary the owner glances at is
  private; the instant it is shared it becomes stranger-facing and inherits both the
  stranger-legibility gap (§1) and the sensitivity floor. **Bound:** felt-to-owner
  surfaces stay counts-not-content, so they are safe to glance even when
  shoulder-surfed; sharing one is a separate, deliberate act, not a default.
- **Calm and felt pull opposite ways.** Too far toward felt is loud; too far toward
  calm is today's invisibility. **Bound:** the test is "ambient record,
  resting-or-summoned, never interrupting." If it grabs attention it has become an
  act and must obey `records-vs-acts.md`; if it cannot be found it has failed this
  doctrine.
- **Legibility ratio.** This file is one-third of the commitment, and the code
  follows in sequence, not all at once. First prove one complete vertical slice on
  the canonical surface (desktop): a real durable mutation in
  `packages/runtime/src/consolidation-cycle.ts` → its signed, provenance-bound
  receipt → a sensitivity-safe summary projection → the calm resting record →
  inspection on explicit reveal → an explicit _authority unchanged_. Then extract the
  projection primitive and one-pass it to web / mobile / spatial
  ([`panels-pattern.md`](panels-pattern.md)). One-pass delivery applies _once the
  package boundary is stable_; the projection primitive does not exist yet, so
  stabilizing it on one surface comes first — the proven order (desktop dogfood, then
  cross-surface) that [`proactive-interior.md`](proactive-interior.md) already
  followed. A drift gate then locks the floor on every felt surface: a no-op forms no
  record; each record maps to a real durable mutation and carries its `MemorySource`
  provenance (so taught and inferred never collapse into one voice); formation cannot
  raise authority ([`memory-never-confers-authority.md`](memory-never-confers-authority.md));
  the same mutation is not double-counted across restart or replay; retirement renders
  honestly; and disclosure obeys the sensitivity ceiling. Until that ships this is the
  spec, not a claim of done.
